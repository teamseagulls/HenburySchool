About Us

Our School

Read More

Current enrollments : 115 students, 41% of students are Indigenous

Staffing ratios: One teacher and one/two Special Education Support Officer for every class

Staffing: 62 staff members, including Health Promoting School Nurse, Aboriginal and Islander Education Worker (AIEW) and School Counsellor Psychologist

13 classrooms on campus and 7 classrooms off campus (including a school house and an Opportunity shop)

Our Student

Read More
To attend Henbury School students need to meet enrolment criteria that consist of an IQ of 70 or below and concurrent deficits in adaptive functioning and requiring intensive support needs within a highly individualised program.

Our Staff

Read More
We have dynamic teaching teams of Classroom Teachers and Special Education Support Officers. Teaching staff are supported by a Principal, Assistant Principal, 2 Senior Teachers and 2 administration staff. Working in a .5 capacity, we have an Aboriginal and Islander Education Worker, a Maintenance Officer, a Health Promoting School Nurse and a School Counsellor. A multi‐disciplinary Therapy Team and A live‐in caretaker support our staff and students as required.
