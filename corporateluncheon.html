Op Shop

Opportunity Knocks, the Henbury Opportunity Shop, is located at 2/69 Aralia Street in Nightcliff.
The idea of an Opportunity Shop came about when we saw a great need for a secure workplace for students who require the opportunity to successfully transition into a work environment. We are very excited with this project, as it has such benefit for students across a broad spectrum of disabilities and a chance for our students to give back and contribute to their community.
